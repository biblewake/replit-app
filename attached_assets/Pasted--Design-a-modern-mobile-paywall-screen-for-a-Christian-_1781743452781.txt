

Design a modern mobile paywall screen for a Christian productivity/accountability app.

The layout should be clean, minimal, and conversion-focused, using a white background and a bright orange primary color (#FF9000) the app orange color.

#### Header Section

At the top center, display a bold headline:

**"How your free trial works"**

Use a large, friendly font with strong visual hierarchy.

#### Trial Timeline Section

Below the title, create a vertical timeline positioned slightly left of center.

The timeline should use a thick rounded vertical line with milestone icons connected along it.

Orange circular icon with a lock symbol.

Title:

**Today**

Description:

"Unlock all the app's features like smart accountability alarms, bible verse analytics, streak tracking, and more."

##### Step 2 — In 2 Days

Orange circular icon with a bell symbol.

Title:

**In 2 Days**

Description:

"We'll send you a reminder that your trial is ending soon."

##### Step 3 — In 3 Days

Green circular icon with a star symbol.

Title:

**In 3 Days**

Description:

"You'll be charged after your 3-day trial unless you cancel anytime before."

The timeline should visually progress downward, with a subtle orange-to-green gradient indicating progression toward subscription activation.
